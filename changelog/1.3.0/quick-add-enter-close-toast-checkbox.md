Quick-add gets sharper capture flows. Enter now commits your thought and closes the overlay — and if you weren't looking at where it landed, a toast confirms it with a "Go there" jump. For a burst, Cmd+Enter commits and keeps the overlay open so you can fire off the next one. And turning a capture into a to-do (`/todo` or Mod+D) now shows the checkbox inline, right where you're typing.
